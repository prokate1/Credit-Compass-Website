Credit Compass Campaign Content

Below are the deliverables from this digital media campaign: a social media strategy, infographic, narrative video ad, and podcast episode. Together they tell the story of Credit Compass and its potential impact on students.

Social Media Strategy

This strategy outlines how Credit Compass would show up on TikTok and Instagram to meet students where they already are. The content plan includes:

  • Short, relatable money scenarios with quick AI-powered tips
  • Pov-style videos from stressed students to “future you”
  • Polls and challenges that invite students to share habits
  • Calls to action that feel encouraging instead of shame-y

Infographic: “Why Students Need a Money Coach”

The infographic summarizes the core problem (money stress + confusing credit) and shows how Credit Compass could help with:

  • Visual breakdown of common student money pain points
  • Simple flow of how the AI coach interacts with a student
  • Key benefits: fewer late fees, better credit habits, more calm

Video Ad: “Less Money Stress, More Future”

This 1–2 minute ad follows a student who feels overwhelmed by bills and credit worries. As Credit Compass steps in, we see:

  • Notifications about upcoming payments and risky spending
  • Friendly tips from the AI coach instead of scary warnings
  • A shift from panic to confidence about the student’s future

To watch in full screen, click the full screen icon in the bottom-right of the video. .

Podcast Episode: “A Day with an AI Money Coach”

This short audio episode walks through a narrative of a student using Credit Compass across a typical week: checking balances, catching potential overdrafts, and getting nudges about credit utilization before things spiral.
