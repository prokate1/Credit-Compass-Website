About This Campaign

This website was created as the final deliverable for an Emerging Technologies course. It pulls together multiple media pieces into one cohesive digital campaign about Credit Compass, a concept for an AI money coach for students.

Campaign Purpose & Narrative

The main goal of this project is to explore how emerging AI tools could be used to make personal finance less confusing and more accessible for college students. Many students juggle classes, jobs, and family responsibilities while also trying to manage bills, credit cards, and loans.

Credit Compass imagines an AI assistant that:

  • Translates complex financial jargon into plain language
  • Gives just-in-time reminders before late fees hit
  • Helps students see how today’s choices affect future credit
  • Coaches rather than judges, building confidence over time

Across the social media strategy, infographic, video ad, and podcast, the narrative shifts from stress and confusion toward clarity and control. The campaign is meant to feel hopeful and realistic, not like a “perfect” fantasy where money problems vanish overnight.

Tools Used

These tools were used to create and assemble the campaign:

  • Canva – infographic design, video ad editing, slide layouts
  • Audio recording/editing – for the podcast (e.g. phone voice recorder / Audacity / Audition)
  • GitHub Pages – hosting this website
  • HTML & CSS – hand-coded layout and styling for the site
